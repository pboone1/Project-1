sophia schaefer

Sophia Schaefer is the girl who can lift you up from any hardship. She is the sweetest, most sentimental person I have ever met. She keeps every birthday card and every handwritten note. She is funny without trying. She will make you feel welcome in every room. I have watched Sophia grow from a quiet nine year old into a beautiful, talented, kind young woman. I am lucky to call her my best friend.
