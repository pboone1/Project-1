noah topmiller

Noah Topmiller is such an interesting guy. He will not answer your texts for weeks at a time, but he will always be there when it counts. From concussions to plagues, he finds a way to show up for his friends. I have known Noah since preschool. I have watched him go through marching band practices, Julius Caesar renditions, honor society presidencies, and worldwide expeditions. I know he will make it far in life and I cannot wait to see where his journey takes him next.
