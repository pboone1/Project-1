I met Ava Lacombe for the first time when I needed another roommate for sophomore year. I met her outside the apartment and she had so much energy and positivity surrounding her. Ava is special. In all her cajun-ness, she lights up a room and makes everyone around her laugh. I never know what I am going to get when being around her and she makes my life that much more interesting.
